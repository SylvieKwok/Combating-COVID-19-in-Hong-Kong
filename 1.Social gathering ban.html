Social gathering ban

• Timeline of social gathering ban
Click the time to see more detailed information

28/9/2020
The Leisure & Cultural Services Department urged people to keep places clean when celebrating the Mid-Autumn Festival.The department reminded people not to fly sky lanterns as they can cause injuries or damage to property and not to burn wax.In public places, group gatherings of more than four people are prohibited and people must wear masks.

8/2/2022
Chief Executive Carrie Lam announced today that the Government will further tighten the social distancing measures in view of the COVID-19 epidemic situation, including prohibiting more than two households from gathering in private premises.

18/10/2022
The Government announced today that it will ease some social distancing measures from October 20 and the maximum number of people allowed in group gatherings in public places will be relaxed to 12.

17/11/2022
The government again relaxed the epidemic prevention measures, from November 17 to reopen the government camping sites and other outdoor recreational facilities; amusement game centers, hair salons, public markets, etc. will not have to take the initiative to check the vaccination pass. Earlier, the number of people in the gathering order was also relaxed from 4 to 12, in line with the maximum number of 12 people a table in restaurants; as for the maximum number of banquets to maintain 240 people; bars, nightclubs, the number of people per table is limited to 6, but attendance at banquets and bars must still be fast negative test.

• The punishiments
Any person who violates the legal requirements of a gathering restriction commits an offence and is liable to a maximum fine of $25,000 and six months imprisonment.
