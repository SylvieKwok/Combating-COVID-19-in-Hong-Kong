Since 2020, the COVID-19 epidemic has raged in Hong Kong, affecting our lives. This virus will not go away easily and will continue to affect us in the future, so

we need to be prepared to continue fighting it. The goal of the website is to make people better aware of the COVID-19 pandemic and provide health reminders

for people to better fight the virus. Finally, there will be relevant academic research for your reference.
