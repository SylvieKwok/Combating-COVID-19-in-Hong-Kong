Three types of health remainder for daily living

• Dining:

Use communal chopsticks
- Use communal chopsticks when eating with friends and family to reduce the chance of cross infection

• Living:

1. Receiving parcels:
- Leave parcels outside the home to avoid contact with the courier
-Use your own pen when signing for parcels
- Dispose of the package as soon as possible after unpacking
-Wash your hands as soon as possible after unpacking

2. Home hygiene:
-Keep your home well ventilated
-Keep the toilets clean
-Wash common household items regularly
-Use alcohol spray on your clothes when you come back home

• Social Activities:

-Wear masks at all times when you go out
-Clean your hands with an alcohol-based hand rub after using public facilities
-Reduce the frequency to visit high-traffic areas
-Maintain social distance
