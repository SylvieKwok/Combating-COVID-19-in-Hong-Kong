Foreseen future under Covid-19

In the foreseen future, Genetic variations influence quickly people's age, but behaviors and life events can also influence epigenetic age — including exposure to infection. Studies suggest that infection with HIV, for example, accelerates the aging and death of immune cells. Some researcher thinks the SARS-CoV-2 virus that causes COVID-19 can also result in chronic inflammation and accelerated biological aging. On the other hand, Laura Fonken, a neuroscientist at the University of Texas at Austin says “Even for people who never get infected with the virus itself, the COVID-19 pandemic has been stressful”, which makes the people age quickly.
