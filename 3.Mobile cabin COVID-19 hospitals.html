Mobile cabin COVID-19 hospitals

• The construction of mobile cabin COVID-19 hospitals in Hong Kong

18/2/2022
The Chief Executive of the Hong Kong SAR, Mrs. Carrie Lam, held a press conference to announce that the construction of a temporary isolation facility, the "mobile cabin COVID-19 hospitals ", will begin in Hong Kong with the assistance of a team from the mainland from February 19th.

22/2/2022
The construction assisted by the mainland government of four mobile cabin COVID-19 hospitals was started.

27/2/2022
The fifth wave of the epidemic in Hong Kong was becoming more and more severe. The isolation facility in Tsing Yi, Hong Kong is expected to be the first to be completed next week and to be put into operation immediately. Tsing Yi "mobile cabin COVID-19 hospitals" covers an area of about 60,000 square meters, can accommodate more than 3,800 beds.
