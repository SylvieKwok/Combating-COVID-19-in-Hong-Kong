The Leave Home Safe APP

LeaveHomeSafe is a digital contact tracing app launched by the Hong Kong Government on 16 November 2020 to conduct contact tracing and mass surveillance in Hong Kong. It can be used as a companion of the Hong Kong Health Code system for registered users. The program can be downloaded for free, allowing users to record their whereabouts.
