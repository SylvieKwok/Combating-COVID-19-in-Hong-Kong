Related Polices

To combat COVID-19, the Hong Kong government has released a number of related policies to contain and reduce the number of people infected with COVID-19, as well as a number of policies to deal with people suffering from COVID-19. The purpose of this page is to introduce the measures taken by the Hong Kong government during the epidemic.
