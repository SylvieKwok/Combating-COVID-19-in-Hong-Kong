Background

The COVID-19 pandemic and its attendant policies, such as lockdowns and movement restrictions, have affected many areas of society and every aspect of everyone's life and work. People are affected differently, depending on their occupation, socioeconomic status, or other circumstances. For example, people's lives have been turned upside down by the demands of real-life human contact and the demands of working remotely.
