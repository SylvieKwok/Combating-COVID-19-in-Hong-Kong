Declaration system of COVID-19

For individuals who tested positive:

When you are tested positive for COVID-19 and are notified via SMS, or obtain a positive Rapid Antigen Test (RAT) result, you will be considered infected. Your household member(s) will be considered as close contact(s). Please provide information to the Centre for Health Protection through the links below:

Nucleic Acid Test: www.chp.gov.hk/cdpi

RAT: www.chp.gov.hk/ratp
