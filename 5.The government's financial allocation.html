The government's financial allocation

The government's financial allocation

On the 23rd, the Financial Secretary of the Hong Kong SAR Government, Mr. Paul Chan, said that the fight against the epidemic is the overriding priority at present, and the SAR Government will use all available forces and resources to combat the epidemic. The SAR government will allocate significantly more resources in the new financial year to fight the epidemic including food supply and sanitation.
